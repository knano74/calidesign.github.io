Hi, I’m Ali Mohammed — a passionate Website Designer who loves turning creative ideas into beautiful, functional, and user-friendly websites.

I specialize in modern web design, using tools like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Figma to bring digital experiences to life. Whether it’s a clean portfolio, a responsive business site, or a bold e-commerce design, I focus on both aesthetics and usability.

Over the years, I’ve learned that great design isn’t just about visuals — it’s about creating experiences that engage visitors and tell a story. I enjoy collaborating with clients to understand their goals and build websites that truly represent their brand.
